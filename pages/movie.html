Relax and unwind with a night of blockbuster hits and cinematic classics at our movie night event, the perfect way to take a break from the tech-heavy excitement of our festival.

And with a fantastic selection of snacks and drinks, you'll have everything you need to fully immerse yourself in the movie experience.
