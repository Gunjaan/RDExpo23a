Web Bot Jam

Join us at our tech fest for a dynamic duo of web development and chatbot making, where you can learn to create innovative digital solutions that transform the way we communicate and interact with technology.
23rd April 11:00AM - 01:00PM

Get ready to put your web skills to the test in our two-round web event competition! You'll have the opportunity to showcase your creativity, technical expertise, and problem-solving skills as you work to develop a stunning and functional website. The participant who receives a particular topic will develop it according to their preferences, along with a teammate who will work on a chatbot in a separate room for the same website. The chatbot developer will receive a 15-minute tutorial before the competition begins. With fantastic prizes up for grabs, including recognition and exposure, this competition is to be noticed.
