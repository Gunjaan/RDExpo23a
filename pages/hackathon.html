Hack UN

Unleash your inner coder and conquer the tech world with the power of innovation at the hackathon!
21st - 22ndApril 06:00PM - 12:00PM

Are you ready to take on the challenge and showcase your skills in a high-energy, competitive environment? In this exciting event, we will be combining the fast-paced and creative energy of a hackathon with the structured and collaborative approach of a Model United Nations conference. You'll have the opportunity to showcase your technical skills and your ability to communicate and negotiate with others. Take advantage of this incredible opportunity to challenge yourself, network with other talented individuals, and potentially win amazing prizes. Our goal for this Hackathon MUN is to work together as a team to tackle a real-world problem and come up with innovative solutions.
